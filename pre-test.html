How It Works

1️⃣ A student selects a course.
2️⃣ A pre-test is conducted to evaluate their skill level.
3️⃣ Based on the test, they are classified as:
  • 🟢 Beginner → Basic videos & materials.
  • 🟡 Intermediate → Moderate-level resources.
  • 🔴 Expert → Advanced content.
4️⃣ The system recommends study materials accordingly.

✨ Innovation: Instead of a static course structure, this adapts to each student’s level, making learning more effective. 🚀
